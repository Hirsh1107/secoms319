We are a company that is dedicated to providing high-quality, reliable marine services to the Lake Minnetonka community. Now two years in operation, our customer base continues to accommodate recreational watercraft users as well as local firms--one such example is Geico Insurance!
As a company providing designated-driving (or "captaining") to our customers, we proudly contribute to a safer lake community by reducing the risk of watercraft accidents.
As we look toward new horizons, we hope to achieve partnership with local restaurants as well as expand our services to other lakeside communities. Please contact us if you have any queries concerning partnership.
Thank you for choosing Float as your marine service provider. We look forward to serving you!
